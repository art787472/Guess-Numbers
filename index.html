It's a number-guessing game.

Inputting four-digit non-repetive numbers, and you will get an outcome of "A" and "B".

Each "A" stands for "right number at right position," while each "B" stands for "right number but wrong position."

For example, if the answer is "2587" and you guessed "3578," you will get "1A 2B" as the outcome.

If having no idea, you can always click on the "random" button which will generate random numbers for you.

use toggle to change the numpad to your prefered layout.
